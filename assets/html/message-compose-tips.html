Cards with red borders (like this) are help cards.

Cards with black borders are message cards.

You can swipe Choice cards left or tap to reject a card.

You can swipe Choice cards right or tap to accept a card.

You can open and close the editor by tapping or by swiping a Choice card upwards.

You can log in and customize your account with the settings tab which is revealed if you tap the top of the screen.

The editor interface is split into three main regions: the Template text (top), the Suggestions (middle), and the Choice card itself (bottom). The Template text and Suggestions are only visible when in edit mode.

If you are logged in (via the settings tab which is revealed if you tap the top of the screen), you may also choose whether you want a Public or a Direct message, and if the latter, you will need to specify a recipient.

The Template text can include regular text as well as synonym expansions, which must be preceded by the 'PHRASE' symbol, like this: PHRASEan_example_phrase

This will look up 'an_example_phrase' in the thesaurus, and (if an entry for that phrase exists) it will randomly select one of the synonyms defined for that phrase, and insert that random definition into the card.

To add to the text on a card, or to edit it, open the editor and tap one of the phrases in the Suggestions region.

When you are satisfied with the text on a card, swipe right or tap to post the card.

The panel that pops up when you tap also allows you to share using Twitter or Facebook

When you are logged in, sharing sends a direct message to another player's mailbox

Alternatively, you can just copy the message to the clipboard
